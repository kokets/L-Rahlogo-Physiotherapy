    Effort ECG (Exercise Stress Test)
    Essential cardiac assessment before rehabilitation.
    Why This Test Matters:
    Safety First

    Identifies cardiac limitations before starting exercise programs.

    Precision Monitoring

    Tracks heart rate, rhythm and BP during exertion.

    Personalized Plans

    Tailors rehab intensity to your cardiac response.

    Confident Movement

    Ensures safe progression in rehabilitation.

    Ideal For:
    Cardiac history Unexplained breathlessness Post-cardiac event Exercise clearance
    What To Expect:
    • Walk/cycle while connected to ECG
    • Supervised by cardiac-trained physios
    • Gradual intensity increase
    • Typically 10-15 minute test
    • Immediate results discussion
    The Result? A customized rehab plan that builds your strength safely—with confidence in every heartbeat.

    Lung Function Test (PFT)
    Essential testing for respiratory conditions and rehab planning.
    What We Measure:
    • Total lung capacity (air volume)
    • Airflow speed (inhalation/exhalation)
    • Oxygen exchange efficiency
    Clinical Uses:
    • Asthma/COPD monitoring
    • Post-COVID assessment
    • Pre/post-surgical evaluation
    • Exercise tolerance testing
    The Testing Process:
    You'll breathe into a spirometer following instructions
    Non-invasive and painless (takes 10-15 minutes)
    Immediate results inform your treatment plan
    A Foundation for Better Breathing: Whether managing chronic conditions or recovering from illness, PFT provides the data we need to optimize your respiratory rehab.
